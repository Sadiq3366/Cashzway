ONTARIO RESIDENTS

IMPORTANT NOTICE:
CHANGES TO ONTARIO PAYDAY LOAN AMOUNTS & PAYMENTS

Starting July 1, 2018, Ontario Payday Loan Regulation will require all borrowers who are entering their third or subsequent payday loan agreement in a 63-day period to have their payments extended over 2-3 installments based on their pay cycle. Additionally, Ontario residents will be eligible for a payday loan up to a maximum of 50% of their net pay amount.
